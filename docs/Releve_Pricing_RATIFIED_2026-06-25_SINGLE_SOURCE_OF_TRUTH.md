## APPLICATION FEE (ratified 2026-06-27 — replaces the retired "verification / background-check fee")
**$30 to apply** to the Roster. **Credited 100% to the member's subscription** if accepted and they join · **fully refunded** if not accepted · **forfeited only if accepted and they decline to subscribe**. It is a commitment / quality filter that funds the council's vetting labor, NOT a charge on anyone who joins — so it does **not** violate the no-tax-on-labor principle. There is **NO background check** (see counsel-ratified no-criminal-history policy). Modeled as **revenue-neutral** (forfeitures not counted as revenue).

**Application fee — scope & framing (ratified 2026-07-11):**
- **Applies to the vetted performer/teacher tier only** (the Professional $149 profile tier). It does **not** apply to Live Pass (no vetting) or to studios (the employer/buyer side).
- **Waived for the Founding 25 choreographers** — they are invited honorees; you do not charge your honorees to apply.
- **Copy rule:** every public touchpoint must **lead with "credited in full toward your membership when accepted, refunded if not accepted,"** never with "pay $30 to apply." The fee is a commitment toward membership, not a charge to work.

## CHARTER COHORT — founding discounts (REVISED 2026-07-11, supersedes the 2026-06-27 "first 25 / 10-10-5")
"Charter" = the founding class: the first paying members who show up early. Distinct from the **Founding 25** (invited choreographer honorees). All Charter discounts are **50% off Year 1 only, full standard rate in Year 2** (not locked-for-life). Per-use fees are not discounted. Delivered via the coupon system.

**Charter Studios — first 50 studios.** 50% off Year 1, across tiers, BUT **Accelerator is capped at ~10 seats** (a scarce, prestigious "Founding Accelerator" cohort — Accelerator is the only tier where the discount leaks real money); the remaining ~40 land on Connect/Growth.
*Year-1 examples: Connect $249 → **$124.50** · Growth $499 → **$249.50** · Accelerator $1,499 → **$749.50**.*

**Charter Faculty — first 50 teacher/performers.** 50% off Year 1 on Professional ($149 → **$74.50**) and Professional·Full ($199 → **$99.50**). Supply is what a cold-start two-sided market needs most, and it's cheap to discount — so be generous here.

**Cohort size = 50 (not 100)** across the board — 50 is a genuine founding cohort, markets as scarce, and caps exposure; 100 would double the leak on the priciest tier.

*Rationale (CFO): a founding discount is customer-acquisition cost, not lost revenue — you're buying founding logos, testimonials, and two-sided liquidity. Concentrate dollars on cheap supply (faculty) and studio trial (entry tiers); cap the expensive Accelerator giveaway. **Modeled Year-1 cost ≈ $15–20K** (≈ $4–5K faculty + ≈ $7.5K the 10 Charter Accelerators + ≈ $7.5K the ~40 Connect/Growth) — vs. $40–85K under an "Accelerator-only / first-100" structure.*
*Supersedes the prior "Founding 5 / 18-month" and the 2026-06-27 "first 25, 10/10/5" versions.*
